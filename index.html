Moringa Cookies

Nutritious & Savory Snack

Product Description

Moringa Cookies with Shredded Chicken are nutritious savory snacks made from fresh moringa leaves and tender shredded chicken, baked to achieve a crispy outside and soft, chewy inside. These cookies provide a healthy and tasty alternative snack rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein.

Ingredients

All-purpose flour, fresh moringa leaves, shredded chicken, butter, sugar, eggs, baking powder

Nutritional Benefits

Rich in vitamins A, B, C, and E, calcium, iron, and plant-based protein.

Storage Instructions

Store in a cool, dry place. Keep away from direct sunlight.
